# Map Layers

Sanitized public taxonomy from China Mission 2026.

This file describes the public map categories without private timing, lodging, routes, or operational traces.

## Robotics and AI Factories

Research layer for humanoids, quadrupeds, robot-data infrastructure, embodied-AI bases, factory expos, industrial robot parks, component suppliers, and scenario-validation systems.

## AI Retail and Product Stores

Research layer for AI hardware stores, robot retail, rental/trial counters, smart devices, wearables, health/pet/office/education products, and support-channel evidence.

## City Clusters

Research layer for Shanghai, Hangzhou, Shenzhen, Suzhou/Taicang, Hefei, and Beijing as different nodes in the physical-AI ecosystem.

## Nature Decompression

Research layer for parks, riversides, gardens, wetlands, temples, water towns, and mountain options used to manage attention.

## Aerospace and Commercial Space

Research layer for museums, public innovation districts, commercial-space parks, satellite manufacturing public material, expo booths, and approved institutional signals.

## Quantum Computing and Education

Research layer for quantum education devices, public cloud/software material, quantum-AI initiatives, public events, and commercialization signals.

## Public Boundary

This public map taxonomy intentionally excludes:

- private route planning;
- travel timing;
- lodging;
- flight or calendar data;
- contact lists;
- people notes;
- raw operational files;
- sensitive coordinates or movement traces.
