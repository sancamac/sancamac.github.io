# Nature Reset Layer

Public synthesis from China Mission 2026.

## Core Frame

Nature was treated as attention infrastructure. Dense technical research needs low-noise counterweights so the next signal remains readable.

## Shanghai Layer

- Sheshan: low-friction hill and forest reset.
- Chenshan Botanical Garden: structured nature, plants, water, slower observation.
- Zhujiajiao: water-town texture and historical decompression.
- West Bund and Xuhui riverside: urban waterline reset near AI/lab corridors.
- Small green spaces: useful when the day is too dense for a full side trip.

## Hangzhou Layer

- West Lake: obvious but correct.
- Lingyin: temple/mountain depth after robotics density.
- Xixi: wetland rhythm and lower-noise walking.

## Suzhou Layer

- Classical gardens: designed attention, compression, stone/water/plant grammar.
- Canal old town: slower urban texture between manufacturing and city research.

## Mountain Layer

Huangshan and broader mountain routes were treated as higher-friction, higher-reward resets. They are not casual add-ons; they only make sense when time and energy support the detour.

## Practical Takeaway

Use nature when the technical stack starts becoming noise. The goal is not to escape the mission, but to recover the mind that can read it.
